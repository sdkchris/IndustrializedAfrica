Small Manufacturing Business Ideas you can start in Africa...

Many of us, African entrepreneurs want to add value to the abondan pull of raw material already available on the continent through manufacturing. Some of us start small prodution units while others expand on existing ones.

In that process, we more or less, encounter the same difficulties and hardships. Especially the expertise and guidance in starting that production unit or small manufacturing business are unavailable from the surrounding communities and other traditional institutions as well, because the concept of manufacturing in Africa is a fairly new one.

Hopefully, Industrialized Africa will help palliate the problem and slowly bridge that knowledge gap and get you started in no time.
